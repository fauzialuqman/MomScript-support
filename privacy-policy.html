Privacy Policy for MomScript

Effective Date: September 7, 2025

Thank you for choosing MomScript. This Privacy Policy describes how MomScript ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Our guiding principle is that your most personal data—your journal entries and mood logs—should belong only to you.

1. Information We Collect

Email Address: When you subscribe to MomScript, we collect your email address to create and manage your account and subscription.

Journal Entries & Mood Logs: This data is stored exclusively on your local device. We do not collect or have access to this information.

Purchase Information: Payments are processed by the Google Play Store. We only receive a confirmation token to verify your subscription.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email and purchase information to provide and maintain the App, manage your subscription, and authenticate you. We use the OpenAI service to generate journal prompts, but no personal data is sent to them.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your email and subscription status are stored securely on our backend (Supabase). Your journal entries are stored in an encrypted database on your device. If you lose your device, this local data will be lost.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated server-side data by visiting our Data Deletion Request page. To delete your local journal data, you must uninstall the App.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@momscript.space
